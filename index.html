Akaltye

Akaltye is a fictional Aboriginal organisation established in 1987 as a resource center led by Aboriginal people for Aboriginal people. Akaltye means knowledge and our goal is to empower communites to reconnect with their cultural identity and heritage.

"This project is important because language holds culture, it helps us to feel connected and understand the people, places and ourselves. "

— Sam Brayne

Arrernte (Aranda) is one of more than 250 Aboriginal languages spoken in Australia. Arrernte is located in Central Northern Territory spanning from MacDonnell Ranges, Mparntwe (Alice Springs) & Hermannsburg.
